NYC Subway Car Project

This environment was created as part of a school project where we were tasked with creating an interior space. I decided to recreate the interior of a New York City subway car. Specifically, I choose to recreate the interior of an R-62 subway car. This project was my first time exporting assets that I created in Maya for use in Unity.

Modeling this project proved to be very difficult as there were very few reference images and blueprints for the inside of the car were either nonexistent or inaccessible to the general public. For many parts of the model, I had to improvise the measurements and estimate the exact proportions of different objects. In all this was a good experience that taught me how to make the best of limited reference resources.
